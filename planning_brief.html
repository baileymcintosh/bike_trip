Bike Trip Planning Brief

Start: Cape Henlopen, DE Target: 30 days Primary finish: LA Fallback: SF or southern completion line

Bottom Line

  • The official Great American Rail-Trail is too long and too incomplete to be your exact 30-day route.
  • Your friend John's ride is the best practical eastern template because it demonstrates real daily stop spacing and pace.
  • The real strategic choice is not “rail-trail or not”; it is mountain 30-day line versus southern completion-first line.

Recommended Decision Point

Denison, Iowa on Day 14 is the meaningful split. If you are on schedule, healthy, and Colorado looks stable, the mountain branch stays alive. If you are already feeling the load, behind schedule, or wary of mountain access, the southern branch is the more realistic completion route.

California Desert Note

Death Valley should not be treated as an improvisation option. If wind, closures, or fatigue force a rethink in eastern California, the correct move is usually to shorten the day, change the overnight stop, or use a more conventional Mojave corridor. Death Valley adds a separate class of exposure and service risk.
